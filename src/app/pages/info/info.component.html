About Us

Welcome to Event Agency! We are a premier event planning and management company specializing in hardcore music, metalcore, and the metal scene of Ukraine.

Our History

Founded in 2013, Event Agency started as a small team of passionate music enthusiasts. Over the years, we have grown into a full-service event management company, organizing hundreds of successful concerts and festivals across Ukraine.

Our Services

  • Music Festivals
  • Band Tours
  • Album Release Parties
  • Merchandise Events

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional music experiences that exceed our clients' expectations. We are dedicated to creativity, innovation, and excellence in every event we plan and execute, bringing the best of hardcore and metal music to our audience.
